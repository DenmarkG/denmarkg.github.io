Delivery Boy

This is a collection of gameplay features I developed as part of my thesis project at the Academy of Art University using Unity. In this project, I've created a 3rd person camera system, Day/Night cycle with transitioning skybox, dialog system, locomotion system with animations, and a simple mission system.


This updated version also features a basic AI and Time Scaling ability. The Stalkers (the Ai Agents) follow a set of waypoints, but will attack the player if he gets too close. Once the player is out of sight, they will continue wandering.


For a detailed list of controls, press the spacebar to pause the game.
